Easy Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon of melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour and sugar.
  2. Add the milk and the egg, then stir well.
  3. Pour in melted butter and mix until smooth.
  4. Heat in a pan and pour in some batter.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook the other side.
